Simple Tech Holdings

An operator-led technology holding company

We build, acquire, and operate technology-driven businesses with a long-term ownership mindset.

What We Are

Simple Holdings is an operating holding company.

We bring businesses together under a shared structure to improve:

Operational discipline

Technology leverage

Governance and capital efficiency

Our role is hands-on. We operate, not observe.

What We Do

We build and acquire technology-led businesses.

We integrate selectively, preserving what makes each company work, while providing centralized capabilities across:

Technology

Finance

Growth

Compliance

Capital is an enabler — operations are the focus.

How We Operate

  • Owner-operators
    We think and act like long-term owners, not financial sponsors.
  • Decentralized execution
    Leadership teams run their businesses with autonomy and accountability.
  • Shared backbone
    Common systems, governance, and strategic support across the group.
  • Measured expansion
    We grow through disciplined acquisitions, not rapid consolidation.
